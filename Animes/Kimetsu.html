Kimetsu no Yaiba

In Kimetsu no Yaiba, Tanjiro, a kind young man who makes his living selling coal, discovers that his family has been slaughtered by a demon. And, to make matters worse, Nezuko, his youngest and only surviving sister, also ended up being transformed into a demon. Overwhelmed with this gloomy reality, Tanjiro decides to become a demon slayer to make his sister human again and to destroy the demon who killed his loved ones.
