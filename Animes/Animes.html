Animes

The history of anime, the animation genre of Japanese origin, begins in the second decade of the 20th century, with a series of short films similar to those found in other countries, largely influenced by Disney works. Some time after World War II , large companies dedicated to both television series and feature films began to emerge, among which stand out in quality are Kyoto Animation and Madhouse.
