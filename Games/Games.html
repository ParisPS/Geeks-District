Games

In the beginning, electronic games were quite simple, with basic graphics and no sound. William A. Higinbotham created one of the first video games, Tennis for Two (“tennis for two”), in 1958.The first arcade game was called Computer Space and it was launched in 1971. Soon other successful games appeared, like Pong, Space invaders and Pac-Man. The first home console also appeared around that time. In 1977, the Atari company launched a console with removable cartridges. Each cartridge ran a different game. With this system, electronic games gained popularity, but people still wanted better images and sounds. Realizing the great interest of the public, many other companies started to develop electronic games. They have improved not only the quality of sound and images, but also the technology used to run games.
